Welcome to Palita and Kevin's wedding on August 26, 2023.
We hope you can join us on our big day when we will celebrate love and commitment.

The wedding will take place at Konditori Lyran in Bredäng, just south of Stockholm, at 5.15 p.m., but we recommend guests to be there at 5 p.m.. The following dinner and party will take place at the same location.
